# Quick Commerce Delivery Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

Power BI dashboard analyzing delivery operations, customer satisfaction, and key performance metrics for a quick commerce business.

## Key Findings
Bangalore generated the highest revenue among all cities.
Electronics had the highest average order value.
Faster delivery partners received better customer ratings.
UPI was the most preferred payment method.
Discounts increased order volume but had varying impact on AOV.

## Recommendations
Increase inventory allocation in top-performing cities.
Promote high-margin categories through targeted campaigns.
Optimize slower delivery routes to improve ratings.
Expand digital payment incentives.
Monitor cancellation hotspots for operational improvements.
