KEY FINDINGS

• Customers with low feature adoption and low NPS contribute most churn.
• Dormant users represent the largest retention opportunity.
• Premium subscribers show the lowest churn risk.
• Revenue concentration exists within high-risk Standard customers.

RECOMMENDED ACTIONS

• Launch re-engagement campaigns for dormant users.
• Improve onboarding to increase feature adoption.
• Prioritize outreach to high-value high-risk customers.
• Reduce support friction for detractor segments.
